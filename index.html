Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Explore everything from courses to career support. Start your journey with confidence today!
What is MCC? +

Mumbai Coding Club is an IT training institute based in Mumbai, India. It offers various courses related to software development, programming, and other technical fields, including Python full stack development, core Python with OOPs, data analytics. Mumbai Coding Club also provides 100% placement assistance to its students, helping them secure jobs in the IT industry.

Who is eligible for MCC? +

Software engineers with any number of years of experience can choose to join the Mumbai Coding Club. Applicants currently in college (3rd or final year of college and integrated students) could consider to joining Mumbai Coding Club , our college student-focused product.

When are the classes held? +

Our classes are held 6 days a week, with 2 batches each day. The first batch runs from 9 AM to 1 PM, and the second batch is from 2 PM to 6 PM. The exact batch assignment will be based on the availability of learners..

Will I get a full-stack developers certification? +

After the end of the course, you will receive a course completion certificate.

What about Placement? +

At Mumbai Coding Club, we train our students to compete with professionals who have 1 to 1.5 years of industry experience. We provide 100% placement assistance, helping our students connect with top companies through our network. Many of our graduates secure positions in reputed firms, startups, and tech companies.
